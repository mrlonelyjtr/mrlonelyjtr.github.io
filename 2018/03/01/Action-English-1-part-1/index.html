Action English 1 (part 1)

Posted on 2018-02-28 | In English

Ep 1

sound engineer 录音师

Rumor has it that…

it is said or it is generally considered that 人们都说…，据说…，据传…
(1) Rumor has it that Andy is a nice guy.
(2) Rumor has it that he is a rather difficult sound engineer.

matter

(not) matter to you 对你（不）重要
It doesn’t matter.
None of that matters.
(1) Your opinion really matters to me.
(2) You matter to me.
(3) It doesn’t matter if Mark isn’t coming. Maybe he just doesn’t do hip-hop you know, so let’s go.

counterfeit/genuine

假的/真的
counterfeit money/counterfeit watches
genuine leather/genuine watches
true love
real friends

(not) worth

…（不）值得
(1) Tibet is definitely worth seeing.
(2) This car isn’t worth the money you paid for it.
(3) Is he worth it?

Ep 2

cartoon 卡通片

Oh, come on!

委婉的表示你对一个人不信任或者不耐烦
(1) Oh, come on, you can do better than that.
come on
1.Hurry up! 你在催促一个人
(1) Come on, let’s get started.
2.加油
(1) Come on! Come on!

Who do we like for this?

凭我们以往的经验，会是谁干的呢？
(1) Who do you like for the soccer game tomorrow night? Brazil or Germany?
(2) Who do you like for the best actress at tomorrow’s Oscar’s?

Oh, gee…

表示事情没想到，惊讶
Gosh!
Oh my God!
Oh, no! 表示对事情非常抱怨，非常不满，发牢骚
Oh, oops! 表示不小心犯了个小错误

Go ahead.

可以，请便
(1) - Can I use the bathroom? - Go ahead.
(2) - Do you mind if I smoke here? - No, go ahead.

Ep 3

action movie 动作片

can’t get over

can’t forget sth 无法忘记…，无法从…的阴影中走出来
(1) The French soccer fans can’t get over the World Cup 2002.
(2) I just cannot get over Liz.
(3) I just can’t get over your decorator.

dwell on

驻扎，留守，引申为能不能不要再想…了，能不能别提及…抓住它不放手 stop thinking about
(1) We shouldn’t dwell on someone’s past.
(2) Hey, Kenny, it’s been five years since you lost the car. You shouldn’t dwell on this forever.

Get down!

1.crunch down a little bit 蹲下
2.face down, flat on the ground 趴下
Freeze 不许动 don’t move, stand still
Duck 把头低下去

What the hell is going on?

究竟出了什么事情？
What is going on? = What happened?/What’s wrong with this?
(1) What the hell are you talking about?
(2) Why the hell should I go there?

have the right to…

有权…
(1) I have the right to stay here as long as I want.
(2) You have the right to keep silent.

Ep 4

preview 预告片

play chicken

just the name of a dangerous game or just a test of courage, usually it’s played with cars
(1) When you drive you’d better not play chicken with bigger cars.

Don’t be a baby!

Don’t complain 不要发牢骚，不要抱怨
(1) Don’t be a baby. Go talk to your boss.
(2) He’s such a baby!
(3) Never, ever be a baby. You know?

keep my edge

stay sharp 让最棒的状态保持下去
lose my edge
(1) I have to speak English every day just in order to keep my edge.
(2) David Beckham is a great soccer player but if he doesn’t practise every day, he’ll lose his edge.

hone your skills

to practise to improve your skills 拼命地实践或者练习，为了提升你的技能
(1) Owning my own computer gives me a chance to hone my computer skills.
(2) Jane’s new job requires her to talk to lots of people. She’s really honing her people’s skills.

Ep 5

screen adaptation 改编

take your pick

you can choose any one you want 随你挑
(1) We have enough money to buy any DVD player this store has so take your pick.

line

在向异性套磁的时候说的话
That was a bad line. 看来刚才向你示爱的方式并不高明
(1) I didn’t give her my phone number because she used a terrible line.
(2) I’m not good at meeting girls because I don’t have any pick up lines.

ladies man

a man who’s good at attracting and talking to women 非常有女人缘的男性
(1) My cousin always has 2 or more women interested in him. He’s a real ladies man!
(2) My brother is a good looking guy and a smooth talker. Really he’s kind of a ladies man!

buy

believe 相信
She’s totally buying it. 她完全买账，完全相信对方
(1) He said he could help me get a job at his company but I don’t buy it. He doesn’t have that much influence.
(2) She told me she loved me but I don’t buy it. I saw her at dinner with her ex- last night.

Ep 6

log keeper 场记

It’s up to you.

It’s your decision. 由你来决定，由你说了算
(1) You can study English or Spanish. It’s up to you.
(2) You can have any sweater you want. It’s up to you. The color’s up to you.

What do you mean?

（懂一个人的话的表面意思，但是不明白为什么这么讲）你究竟是什么意思？
(1) What do you mean we have no more milk. I just bought some yesterday.

Can I get a minute?

hold on, just wait a minute 等以下好吗，能不能等我一下
(1) Gosh, I think I hurt myself. Can I get a minute?
(2) Hey, Michael, you walk too fast. I’m tired. Can I get a minute?
Just a minute. （打电话的时候）您稍等，我去给你找…

What’s the matter?

（表达对别人的关怀、问候）你怎么了？
What’s the problem? 出什么问题没有？
What’s got into you?
What’s going on?
(1) Why are you crying? What’s the matter?
(2) You are acting strangely. What’s the matter with you?

propose

just to ask someone to marry 求婚
propose to sb
(1) Did Ed propose to Jane, yet?
(2) How did you propose to your wife?
get engaged to 订婚
(1) A got engaged to B.
get married to 结婚
(1) A got married to B.

Ep 7

crew 剧组

bust my ass

1.make somebody’s life difficult or just give somebody trouble 让…的日子不好过，让…难堪
Stop busting my ass. 别再为难我了
(1) I wish the directors just would stop, you know, busting my ass because I already have lots of problems.
2.work hard 拼命地、努力地工作
(1) Every day I work, I bust my ass so my family can live well.

smooth

1.to be cool, to be skilled at 对…很擅长，很有一套
(1) He’s very smooth with women because he has dates every weekend.
2.just describe people, the kind of person who just can makes a difficult task look easy 形容一种人，这种人可以把很复杂的事情变得很简单的，一出手就有
(1) Somebody is a smooth.
3.be good at influencing people 指一种人，非常有感染力，非常有渲染力，可以很轻易得说服别人
(1) He has a very smooth manner, polite, interesting and a good talker.

sneak

move silently without being seen 走路的时候非常得小心，以便于不被人察觉，潜入
(1) If I come home late, I sneak into my house so I don’t wake anyone up.

head

go somewhere 去…地方（用将来时或现在进行时都可以）
(1) I’m so tired, I’m heading home.
(2) Are you heading to class later?
行为动词+home/here/there时，要把to给省略掉

memorize

to remember something 记住…，背下…
(1) I have to memorize forty vocabulary words today.
