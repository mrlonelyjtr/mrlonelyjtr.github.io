Action English 1 (part 1)

Posted on 2018-02-28 | In English

Ep 1

sound engineer 录音师

Rumor has it that…

it is said or it is generally considered that 人们都说…，据说…，据传…
(1) Rumor has it that Andy is a nice guy.
(2) Rumor has it that he is a rather difficult sound engineer.

matter

(not) matter to you 对你（不）重要
It doesn’t matter.
None of that matters.
(1) Your opinion really matters to me.
(2) You matter to me.
(3) It doesn’t matter if Mark isn’t coming. Maybe he just doesn’t do hip-hop you know, so let’s go.

counterfeit/genuine

假的/真的
counterfeit money/counterfeit watches
genuine leather/genuine watches
true love
real friends

(not) worth

…（不）值得
(1) Tibet is definitely worth seeing.
(2) This car isn’t worth the money you paid for it.
(3) Is he worth it?

Ep 2

cartoon 卡通片

Oh, come on!

委婉的表示你对一个人不信任或者不耐烦
(1) Oh, come on, you can do better than that.
come on
1.Hurry up! 你在催促一个人
(1) Come on, let’s get started.
2.加油
(1) Come on! Come on!

Who do we like for this?

凭我们以往的经验，会是谁干的呢？
(1) Who do you like for the soccer game tomorrow night? Brazil or Germany?
(2) Who do you like for the best actress at tomorrow’s Oscar’s?

Oh, gee…

表示事情没想到，惊讶
Gosh!
Oh my God!
Oh, no! 表示对事情非常抱怨，非常不满，发牢骚
Oh, oops! 表示不小心犯了个小错误

Go ahead.

可以，请便
(1) - Can I use the bathroom? - Go ahead.
(2) - Do you mind if I smoke here? - No, go ahead.

Ep 3

action movie 动作片

can’t get over

can’t forget sth 无法忘记…，无法从…的阴影中走出来
(1) The French soccer fans can’t get over the World Cup 2002.
(2) I just cannot get over Liz.
(3) I just can’t get over your decorator.

dwell on

驻扎，留守，引申为能不能不要再想…了，能不能别提及…抓住它不放手 stop thinking about
(1) We shouldn’t dwell on someone’s past.
(2) Hey, Kenny, it’s been five years since you lost the car. You shouldn’t dwell on this forever.

Get down!

1.crunch down a little bit 蹲下
2.face down, flat on the ground 趴下
Freeze 不许动 don’t move, stand still
Duck 把头低下去

What the hell is going on?

究竟出了什么事情？
What is going on? = What happened?/What’s wrong with this?
(1) What the hell are you talking about?
(2) Why the hell should I go there?

have the right to…

有权…
(1) I have the right to stay here as long as I want.
(2) You have the right to keep silent.
